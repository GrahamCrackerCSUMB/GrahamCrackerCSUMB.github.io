Role Responsibilities
Scrum Master The person responsible for the team’s effectiveness. They coach team members, remove obstacles for the team, keep everyone on time, and coordinate with stakeholders.
Product Owner The person responsible for maximizing the value of the product that the Scrum Team works on. They come up with the Product Goal and create product backlog items.
Developers The people doing the work! They are responsible for creating a plan for the Sprint, adapting as needed, and are responsible for the quality of their output.
